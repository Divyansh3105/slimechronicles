Factions & Organizations

Political powers, alliances, and ruling forces shaping the world of Jura Tempest.

Jura Tempest Federation

Nation

A monster-led nation founded by Rimuru Tempest, built on coexistence, strength, and innovation.

Leadership

  • Supreme Leader: Rimuru Tempest
  • Executives: Benimaru, Shion, Shuna, Souei
  • Capital: Tempest City

Goals & Ideology

  • Create a world where monsters and humans coexist
  • Establish peaceful trade relationships
  • Protect all citizens regardless of race

Allies & Enemies

  • Allies: Dwargon, Blumund, Ingrassia, Demon Lords
  • Enemies: Western Holy Church, Clayman (former)

Key Events

  • Foundation after Goblin Village naming
  • Falmuth War and Rimuru's awakening
  • Walpurgis Council participation

Armed Nation of Dwargon

Nation

A powerful dwarf kingdom known for superior craftsmanship, engineering, and military technology.

  • Leader: King Gazel Dwargo
  • Location: Mountains northwest of Jura
  • Population: Primarily dwarves
  • Specialty: Smithing, engineering, weapons
  • Status: Allied with Tempest
  • Notable: Advanced magical technology

Kingdom of Ingrassia

Nation

The strongest human nation with central location, wealth, and political influence.

  • Location: West of Jura Forest
  • Status: Wealthy trading nation
  • Notable: Free Guild headquarters
  • Education: Freedom Academy
  • Geography: Central, well-defended
  • Relations: Friendly with Tempest

Holy Empire Ruberios

Nation

A theocratic empire ruled by the Western Holy Church, one of the most powerful military forces.

  • Leader: Holy Emperor Louis Valentine
  • True Ruler: Luminous Valentine (secret)
  • Religion: Luminism
  • Military: Extremely powerful
  • Status: Politically neutral
  • Influence: Western nations

Eastern Empire

Nation

Officially the Sovereign Alliance of Nasca Namrium Ulmeria Empire, the strongest military nation before Tempest's rise.

  • Official Name: Sovereign Alliance of Nasca Namrium Ulmeria
  • Status: Largest military power
  • Ambition: World conquest
  • Target: Ruberios & other nations
  • Threat Level: Extremely high
  • Relations: Hostile to most

Kingdom of Blumund

Nation

A small nation bordering Jura Forest, known for information trading and early alliance with Tempest.

  • Size: Small nation
  • Location: Borders Jura Forest
  • Defense: Limited military
  • Specialty: Information trading
  • Status: Allied with Tempest
  • Strategy: Diplomatic survival

Animal Kingdom Eurazania

Nation

A beastman kingdom ruled by Demon Lord Carrion, known for advanced agriculture but limited technology.

  • Leader: Demon Lord Carrion
  • Population: Beastmen
  • Specialty: Advanced farming techniques
  • Technology: Relatively backward
  • Military: Three Beastketeers
  • Relations: Diplomatic with Tempest

Kingdom of Farmenas

Nation

Successor state to the fallen Kingdom of Falmuth, established after Tempest's intervention.

  • Leader: King Youm Farmenas
  • Predecessor: Kingdom of Falmuth
  • Advisor: Edmaris Falmuth (former king)
  • Status: Under Tempest influence
  • Economy: Rebuilding trade routes
  • Population: Former Falmuth citizens

Octagram (Demon Lords)

Council

The ruling council of eight Demon Lords that maintains global balance and oversees continental politics.

Leadership

  • Members: Rimuru, Guy Crimson, Milim, Luminous
  • Also: Dagruel, Leon, Ramiris, Dino
  • Chairperson: Rotates by meeting

Goals & Purpose

  • Maintain world equilibrium and balance
  • Prevent large-scale conflicts
  • Oversee territorial disputes

Territory & Influence

  • Scope: Global governance authority
  • Regions: Magic Continent divisions
  • Power: Supreme political authority

Key Events

  • Rimuru's admission as True Demon Lord
  • Clayman's trial and execution
  • Walpurgis Council meetings

Free Guild

Guild

International adventurer's guild with headquarters in Ingrassia, managing quests and adventurer rankings.

  • Headquarters: Kingdom of Ingrassia
  • Function: Adventurer management
  • Services: Quest distribution, rankings
  • Scope: International operations
  • Neutrality: Non-political stance
  • Influence: Cross-border activities

Western Holy Church

Military

Religious military organization based in Ruberios, violently opposed to monster nations and Demon Lords.

  • Base: Holy Empire Ruberios
  • Ideology: Anti-monster extremism
  • Target: Jura Tempest Federation
  • Influence: Western nations
  • Methods: Military intervention
  • Status: Hostile to Tempest

Cerberus

Guild

Secret criminal organization based in the Eastern Empire, led by Yuuki Kagurazaka, handling both legal and illegal operations.

  • Leader: Yuuki Kagurazaka
  • Base: Eastern Empire
  • Operations: Legal & illegal work
  • Market: Black market dominance
  • Backing: Nobles and major powers
  • Counterpart: Freedom Association

Kingdom of Falmuth (Fallen)

Nation

Former trading kingdom that was dissolved after launching a failed war against Tempest to maintain trade monopoly.

  • Status: Dissolved nation
  • Former Role: Gateway to western nations
  • Downfall: War against Tempest
  • Cause: Trade route disruption
  • Successor: Kingdom of Farmenas
  • Legacy: Economic warfare consequences

Goblin Tribes

Race

The original inhabitants of Jura Forest who became the foundation of Tempest Federation.

Leadership

  • Chief: Rigurd (Goblin Lord)
  • General: Rigur
  • Population: ~200 evolved goblins

Goals & Role

  • Serve as Tempest's administrative backbone
  • Protect the Great Jura Forest
  • Maintain traditional goblin culture

Key Events

  • First encounter with Rimuru
  • Mass naming and evolution
  • Formation of Tempest Federation

Fanged Wolf Clan

Race

Powerful wolf monsters led by Ranga, serving as Tempest's elite cavalry and scouts.

Leadership

  • Alpha: Ranga (Star Wolf)
  • Pack Size: ~100 wolves
  • Territory: Great Jura Forest

Goals & Role

  • Serve as Rimuru's personal mount and guard
  • Provide reconnaissance and mobility
  • Protect forest territories

Key Events

  • Initial conflict with goblin village
  • Submission to Rimuru after Veldora's aura
  • Evolution through naming

Lizardmen Tribes

Race

Marsh-dwelling reptilian humanoids led by Gabiru, known for their adaptability and loyalty.

Leadership

  • Leader: Gabiru (Dragonewt)
  • Former Chief: Abiru
  • Population: Several hundred

Goals & Role

  • Integrate into Tempest society
  • Maintain marsh territories
  • Provide aquatic military support

Key Events

  • Orc Lord crisis involvement
  • Gabiru's rebellion and exile
  • Integration into Tempest Federation
